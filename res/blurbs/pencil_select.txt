Shortcut: {T}

Use the pencil select tool for pixel-perfect selection.
It can be used to select a single pixel by clicking on it,
or clicked and dragged in additive or subtractive mode to act
as a precise selection pencil or eraser.

Simple selection: {Click}

Additive selection: {Ctrl + Click & Drag}
Adds the result of the selection to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: {Ctrl + S + Click & Drag}
Removes the result of the selection from the existing
selection.
