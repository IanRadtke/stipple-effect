Shortcut: {sc:SET_TOOL_BOX_SELECT}

Use the box select tool to make rectangular selections.
This is a convenient way to quickly enclose a desired selection area.

Simple selection: {Click & Drag}

Additive selection: {Ctrl + Click & Drag}
Adds the bounds of the box to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: {S + Click & Drag}
Removes the bounds of the box from the existing selection.

If the pixel grid is on, hold {Shift} while selecting to snap the selection to the pixel grid.
