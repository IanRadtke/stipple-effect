Shortcut: {X}

Use the box select tool to make rectangular selections. This is a convenient way to quickly enclose
a desired selection area.

Simple selection: {Click & Drag}

Additive selection: {Ctrl + Click & Drag}
Adds the result of the selection to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: {Ctrl + S + Click & Drag}
Removes the result of the selection from the existing selection.
