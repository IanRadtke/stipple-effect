Shortcut: {F}

Use fill to fill adjacent pixels of the same or
similar color as the initial pixel that was clicked.
Adjust the tolerance of fill to increase or decrease the
threshold of the similarity of the colors.

Primary color: {Left Click}
Secondary color: {Right Click}

Simple fill: {Click}

Global fill: {Shift + Click}
Fills all pixels in the image that satisfy the color
tolerance of the initial pixel, regardless of adjacency.
