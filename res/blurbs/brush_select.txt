Shortcut: {V}

Behaves like the brush, but for selection.

Simple selection: {Click}

Additive selection: {Ctrl + Click & Drag}
Adds the result of the operation to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: {S + Click & Drag}
Removes the result of the operation from the existing selection.

Increment/decrement brush width: {Arrow Keys} or {Shift + Scroll Wheel}
