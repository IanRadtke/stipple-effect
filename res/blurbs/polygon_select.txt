Shortcut: {Y}

Define a polygonal selection area by placing vertices. Adjacent vertices form edges that define
the bounds of the selection. To close the polygon, place a vertex down on the same pixel where
the shape began.

Place vertex: {Left Click}
Close polygon: {Shift + Left Click}
Remove previous vertex: {Right Click}
Reset: {Middle Click}

Additive selection: Hold {Ctrl} while placing vertices
Adds the polygon to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: Hold {S} while placing vertices
Removes the polygon from the existing selection.
