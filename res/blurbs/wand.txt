Shortcut: {W}

Use the wand to select adjacent pixels of the same or similar color as the initial pixel that was
clicked. Adjust the tolerance of the wand to increase or decrease the threshold of the similarity of
the colors.

Simple selection: {Click}

Additive selection: {Ctrl + Click}
Adds the result of the wand wave to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: {S + Click}
Removes the result of the wand wave from the existing selection.

Global selection: {Shift + Click}
Selects all pixels in the image that satisfy the color tolerance of the initial pixel, regardless of
adjacency.

Adjust tolerance: {Arrow Keys} or {Shift + Scroll Wheel}
