Shortcut: {W}

Use the wand to select adjacent pixels of the same or
similar color as the initial pixel that was clicked.
Adjust the tolerance of the wand to increase or decrease
the threshold of the similarity of the colors.

Simple selection: {Click}

Additive selection: {Ctrl + Click}
Adds the result of the selection to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: {Ctrl + S + Click}
Removes the result of the selection from the existing
selection.

Global selection: {Shift + Click}
Selects all pixels in the image that satisfy the color
tolerance of the initial pixel, regardless of adjacency.

Adjust tolerance: {Arrow Keys}
