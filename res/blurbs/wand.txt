Shortcut: {W}

Tap the wand to select adjacent pixels of the same or
similar color as the initial pixel that was tapped.
Adjust the tolerance of the wand to be more or less
discerning of the similarity of the colors.

Simple selection: {Click}

Additive selection: {Ctrl + Click}
Adds the result of the selection to the existing selection.

Subtractive selection: {Ctrl + Shift + Click}
Removes the result of the selection from the existing
selection.

Global selection: {Shift + Click}
Selects all pixels in the image that meet the color
tolerance of the initial pixel regardless of adjacency.
