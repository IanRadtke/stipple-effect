Shortcut: {sc:SET_TOOL_BRUSH}

A pixel-perfect paint brush with no anti-aliasing

Primary color: {Left Click & Drag}
Secondary color: {Right Click & Drag}

Dither mode: {Ctrl + Click & Drag}
Alternates on painted pixels between the primary and secondary colors in a checkerboard pattern

Blend mode: {Shift + Click & Drag}
Paints with a color that is the RGBA average of the primary and secondary colors

Noise mode: {Ctrl + Shift + Click & Drag}
Paints each pixel with a random color that lies between the RGBA bounds of the primary and secondary
colors.

Increment/decrement brush width: {Arrow Keys} or {Shift + Scroll Wheel}
