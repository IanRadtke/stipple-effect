Reflect selection bounds about x-axis: {Shift + 4}
Reflect selection contents about x-axis: {Ctrl + Shift + 4}

Reflect the selection about the x-axis. Pressing the button
will reflect the {bounds} or {contents} based on the current
{selection mode}. To specify {bounds reflection}, ensure the
{move selection} tool is equipped. To specify {contents},
ensure the {pick up selection} tool is equipped.
