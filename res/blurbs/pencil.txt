Shortcut: {P}

A pixel-perfect pencil that draws one pixel at a time

Primary color: {Left Click & Drag}
Secondary color: {Right Click & Drag}

Dither mode: {Ctrl + Click & Drag}
Alternates on drawn pixels between the primary and
secondary colors in a checkerboard pattern

Blend mode: {Shift + Click & Drag}
Draws with a color that is the RGBA average of the
primary and secondary colors

Random bounded mode: {Ctrl + Shift + Click & Drag}
Draws each pixel with a random color that lies between
the RGBA bounds of the primary and secondary colors.
